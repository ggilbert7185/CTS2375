  • MAR 11, 2020

    The World Health Organization declared that the coronavirus outbreak “can be characterized as a pandemic,” which is defined as worldwide spread of a new disease for which most people do not have immunity.

WHAT IS COVID-19?

COVID-19 is a novel coronavirus that started its trail of terror right as we were ushering in a new year, 2020. COVID-19 goes by many names, notably: 2019-nCoV, 2019 Novel Coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2. It is a type of coronavirus that targets the host's upper-respiratory system. Symptoms for this virus come in different severity levels, ranging from mild to life-threatening. Additionally, it is possible for a host to be asymptomatic. Further information on symptoms can be found HERE.
